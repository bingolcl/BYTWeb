About Us

It is our mission at BYT Engineering Corp. to provide expert solutions to our clients' complex automation problems while emphasizing collaboration, quality, and innovation.

Our engineers possess wide application capabilities, ample experience in project management, and comprehensive technical support in English, Spanish, Mandarin, and Tagalog.

With nearly 25 years working in western Canada, and over 60 years of combined experience in heavy industry applications, our team is equipped to bring your project from its planning stage to it's completion, and further.
