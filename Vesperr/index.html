About Us

BYT Engineering Corporation is a provider of complex automation solutions.

BYT can plan-design-build-commission and troubleshoot the equipment of your projects. Its Engineers have over 60 years of combined experience in heavy industry applications.

Our Canada operation is headquartered in Coquitlam, British Columbia.
